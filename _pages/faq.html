Frequently asked questions

Do I need to know how to speak Spanish in order to help? No. Children above six or seven years old speak English because of having to attend public school. The parents, however, are rarely bi-lingual.

With what organization are you affiliated? Sophia Berger was sent out by her home church, Victory Mountain Grace Brethren Chapel in Dryhill, Kentucky. Currently she is not under the auspices of a certain mission organization. When in Michigan (February to November) she attends Coloma Bible Church and on occasion, Primera Iglesia Bautista Hispana Midway in Hartford.

What curriculum do you use? Bible lessons and Day Camp materials are taken from Child Evangelism Fellowship, Firm Foundations from New Tribes Mission, resources from Good Seed International and Answers In Genesis.
